# Heicode Legal Notice & Interpretation

This document explains the scope of application and interpretation rules for Heicode's legal documents, product descriptions, terms of service, privacy policy, security notices, secret vault notice, and commercial plan descriptions.

## Scope of Application

- This document applies to legal and product policy descriptions displayed on the Heicode website, documentation site, client applications, Manager, Agent platform, related services, plan descriptions, product materials, and support channels.
- This document, together with the Privacy Policy, Secret Vault Notice, Terms of Service, Plan Descriptions, and other specific rules, together constitute Heicode's product policy statements to users and customers.
- If a customer has separately signed an order, enterprise agreement, data processing agreement, non-disclosure agreement, service-level agreement, or supplementary terms with Heicode or a related entity, the signed agreement takes precedence.

## Right of Interpretation & Order of Precedence

- To the extent permitted by law, Heicode reserves the right to interpret its product policies, plan descriptions, feature descriptions, billing methodology, resource usage rules, secret vault rules, security requirements, and service boundaries.
- This right of interpretation does not exclude or limit any mandatory rights users are entitled to by law, nor does it exclude mandatory requirements of regulators, judicial authorities, or applicable law.
- If this document is inconsistent with a signed agreement, the signed agreement shall prevail.
- If this document is inconsistent with the current official terms of a model provider, cloud provider, payment provider, deployment provider, or other third-party service, the corresponding third-party service's official terms shall prevail.
- If there is a discrepancy between website content, marketing materials, product descriptions, presentations, help documentation, and system prompts, the signed agreement, formal terms of service, privacy policy, order, or the latest officially published document shall prevail.

## AI Output & Human Review

- Heicode provides AI-assisted software lifecycle capabilities; model outputs, Agent execution results, code suggestions, deployment suggestions, security suggestions, and legal or financial text may contain errors, be incomplete, duplicated, unsafe, or unsuitable for a user's business context.
- Before relying on AI output, users should perform human review, testing, examination, and any necessary professional judgment.
- In medical, legal, financial, compliance, security, production deployment, data migration, and other high-risk scenarios, AI output should only be used as supporting material, not as the sole basis for decisions.

- Users may only connect repositories, documents, cloud resources, accounts, SK packages, data, and secrets that they are authorized to use.
